Mesa, Arizona

A highly populated, widespread city, Mesa is the third-largest city in Arizona and lies within Maricopa County. This college city has much to offer for visitors -- from national parks to art galleries to sports stadiums; there's something for everyone. A city rich in history, it is home to archaeological sites such as the Mesa Grande Ruins and the Park of the Canals.
